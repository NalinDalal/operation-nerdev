═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                            DETAILED BREAKDOWN
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: COLD OUTREACH
├── Goal: Get reply, start conversation
├── Template: Personalized email
├── Follow-up: 3-5 days if no reply
└── Next: Discovery call if interested

STEP 2: DISCOVERY CALL (30 mins)
├── Goal: Understand scope, budget, timeline
├── Questions: Use discovery-call-questions.md
├── Notes: Document key points
└── Next: Send proposal within 2 days

STEP 3: PROPOSAL SENT
├── Format: PDF + summary email
├── Valid: 14 days
├── Includes: Scope, timeline, pricing, terms
└── Next: Follow up in 3-5 days

STEP 4: CONTRACT SIGNED + ADVANCE
├── Amount: 30% upfront
├── Non-refundable advance
├── Signed contract required
└── Next: Schedule kickoff

STEP 5: KICKOFF CALL
├── Agenda: Reconfirm scope, communication plan
├── Access: Share credentials, set up communication
├── Timeline: Confirm milestones
└── Next: Start development

STEP 6: DEVELOPMENT
├── Milestone 1: Requirements finalization
├── Milestone 2: MVP delivery (40% due)
├── Regular updates: Weekly or bi-weekly
├── Issue tracking: Notion/Jira/Trello
└── Next: QA before delivery

STEP 7: QA & TESTING
├── Client testing period
├── Bug fixes (included in quote)
├── 2 rounds of revisions
└── Next: Final delivery

STEP 8: FINAL DELIVERY
├── 30% final payment before handoff
├── All deliverables transferred
├── Documentation provided
└── Next: Support period

STEP 9: ONGOING SUPPORT (Optional)
├── Monthly retainer options
├── Bug fixes (post-launch)
├── Maintenance & updates
└── Relationship building


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
