# Invoice Follow-up Sequence

*Use for overdue invoices*

---

## Email 1: Friendly Reminder (Due Date)

**Subject:** Quick reminder — Invoice #[NUMBER] due today

**Body:**

Hi [Client Name],

Hope you're doing well!

Just a friendly reminder that Invoice #[NUMBER] for [Project Name] is due today ([DUE DATE]).

Total amount: [₹/$][AMOUNT]
[Payment link or bank details]

No action needed if you've already sent payment — just wanted to make sure it didn't slip through.

Thanks!
[Your Name]

---

## Email 2: Firm Reminder (7 Days Overdue)

**Subject:** Reminder — Invoice #[NUMBER] is 7 days overdue

**Body:**

Hi [Client Name],

I wanted to follow up on Invoice #[NUMBER] which was due on [DUE DATE].

Amount: [₹/$][AMOUNT]

I understand things can get busy, but wanted to flag that the payment is now 7 days overdue. Per our contract, a 2% late fee applies after 7 days.

Could you please confirm the status of payment? Happy to hop on a quick call if anything has changed.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

---

## Email 3: Final Notice (14 Days Overdue)

**Subject:** Final notice — Invoice #[NUMBER] + late fee

**Body:**

Hi [Client Name],

This is a final notice regarding Invoice #[NUMBER], now 14 days overdue.

**Invoice Details:**
- Original amount: [₹/$][AMOUNT]
- Days overdue: 14
- Late fee (2% per week): [₹/$][FEE]
- **Total due now: [₹/$][TOTAL]**

Per our contract (Section [X]), payment not received within 7 days incurs a 2% late fee per week.

Please send payment within 7 days to avoid further action. Payment details:
[BANK DETAILS / PAYMENT LINK]

If there's an issue with the work or invoice, please let me know immediately so we can resolve it.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

---

## Email 4: Last Resort (21+ Days Overdue)

**Subject:** Urgent — Invoice #[NUMBER] and next steps

**Body:**

Hi [Client Name],

I'm reaching out one last time regarding Invoice #[NUMBER], now 21 days overdue.

Total due: [₹/$][TOTAL] (including late fees)

At this point, I am required to suspend work on [any ongoing project] until payment is received, per our signed contract.

If payment is not received within [X days], I will need to proceed with the termination clause in our contract.

Please treat this as urgent and confirm payment timeline.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

---

## Best Practices

- Keep all emails professional and non-confrontational
- Always include invoice number and amount
- Send Email 1 on the due date (not after)
- Track in a spreadsheet or Notion
- Consider a quick WhatsApp follow-up alongside emails
- After Email 4, consult contract termination clause
