# Discovery Call Questions

*Use this to extract scope and avoid underquoting*

---

## 1. About the Project

**The Problem**
- What problem are you trying to solve?
- Why is this important now?
- What happens if you don't solve it?

**The Solution**
- What does success look like?
- Who are the end users?
- What platforms (web/mobile/both)?

**The Scope**
- What's in scope for Phase 1?
- What's explicitly out of scope?
- Any nice-to-haves for later?

---

## 2. About the Client

**Timeline**
- When do you need this live?
- Any hard deadlines (launch event, funding round)?
- What happens if we miss the deadline?

**Budget**
- What's the budget for this project?
- Is there flexibility if scope changes?
- Any recurring budget for maintenance?

**Decision Making**
- Who makes the final decisions?
- Who will provide feedback?
- Are there other stakeholders to align with?

---

## 3. Technical Details

**Existing Setup**
- Do you have a domain/hosting?
- Any existing codebase or assets?
- Any third-party tools to integrate?

**Data & Users**
- How many users expected?
- Any sensitive data (PII, payments)?
- Need user accounts/authentication?

**Design**
- Do you have brand guidelines?
- Need design help?
- Any competitor sites to reference?

---

## 4. Process

**Communication**
- What's your preferred way to communicate?
- How often do you want updates?
- Who is the main point of contact?

**Feedback**
- How will you provide feedback?
- What's the turnaround time?
- Any approval processes?

---

## 5. Wrap Up

- Any questions for me?
- What's the next step from your side?
- When can I send the proposal?

---

*Tip: Take notes during the call. Send a summary email after with key points and next steps.*
